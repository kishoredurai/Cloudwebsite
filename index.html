About Us

The key objective of the CLOUD LAB is to develop fundamental, next-generation cloud and storage technologies that support a true utility-driven service - oriented computing

  • Devlop indigenous cloud solutions to stay ahead in the field of information technology
  • Deliver training to student and faculties to improve their IT solution development skills
  • Secure consultancy service by reaching out to the public

Cloud computing is the on-demand availability of computer system resources, especially data storage and computing power, without direct active management by the user.
